About Pacing Studio

Pacing Studio started as a simple idea: give runners and coaches a clean, accurate alternative to clunky online calculators. The tools here are built to match how real sessions are planned – from split-by-split race pacing to track-type conversions – with a focus on clarity and speed.

Everything is tuned for track, road, and XC use: distances that make sense, lap-accurate splits, and conversions that reflect how athletes actually race across different tracks. The goal is simple: help you turn goal times into clear, confident plans.
